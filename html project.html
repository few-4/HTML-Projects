Why I Love It

I love my hoppy as it opens up a galaxy of aesthetic opportunities in 2D or 3D media. Also, it helps in expanding my imagination and makes me feel alive.

How to get started

Steps for beginners :

  1. Choose a digital art software
  2. Create a sketch
  3. Design on several layers
  4. Draw with colors
  5. Delete the sketch layers
  6. Add final details
  7. Save your digital illustration

Description: Digital art is a simple term for a big topic. It spans everything from iPhone sketches to electronic paintings, 8-bit throwbacks and mind-blowing CGI.
